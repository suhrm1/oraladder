The high score ranking is the "who is who" of the most successful OpenRA Ladder players. To make the scoreboard a player must compete in the ranked ladder seasons and score at least a position among the top 10 percent (relative to the number of active players in the season). To climb to the top of the high score table, players need to claim 1st place in as many individual seasons as possible.
